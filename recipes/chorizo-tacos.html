Chorizo Street Tacos



Here we will show you how to create a delcious and simple lunch dish which is common in Mexican street food culture. Chorizo is a staple of seasoned pork meat which is common in Mexican households. Here we will show you how to easily prepare street styled Chorizo Tacos which can be enjoyed by friends and family.

Ingrediants Needed

  • 1 chorizo sausage link, casing removed and meat crumbled
  • 2 tablespons of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped onion or to liking
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh cilantro or to liking

Directions

Step 1

Mix chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce together in a bowl

Step 2

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer chorizo to a plate, reserving the grease in the skillet.

Step 3

Heat tortillas in the reserved grease in skillet over medium heat until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Stack 2 tortillas on top of each other for each taco. Fill with chorizo, onion, and cilantro.
